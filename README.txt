INFO6019 Physics and Simulation (Project 2)

The purpose of this project is to familiarize ourselves with physics simulations rendered with 3D graphics, by making some fireworks!  Enjoy a fun fireworks show by pressing 1, 2 and 3 to launch different kinds of fireworks.  

CONTROLS
W/A/S/D	- Move the camera around the scene
Q/E	- Move the camera up and down in the scene
Mouse	- Moving the mouse moves the camera's target allowing you to look around
1/2/3	- Launch a stage 1 firework of different types

Built and compiled using Visual Studios 2019 in Debug and Release in x64.
